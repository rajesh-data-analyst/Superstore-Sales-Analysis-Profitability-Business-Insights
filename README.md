# 📊 Superstore Sales Analysis | Profitability & Business Insights

## 🔍 Overview
This project analyzes retail sales data to understand profitability, discount impact, and business performance across categories, regions, and customer segments.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Identify profit-driving and loss-making areas  
- Analyze discount impact on profitability  
- Understand customer and regional trends  

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
SQL, Excel, Python (Pandas)

---

## 📁 Dataset
- ~10,000 records  
- Includes sales, profit, discount, category, region, and customer data  

---

## ⚙️ Process
- Cleaned and prepared data  
- Performed exploratory data analysis (EDA)  
- Analyzed sales, profit, and discount relationships  
- Compared performance across categories and regions  

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Technology category generated highest profit  
- Furniture category showed losses (especially Tables)  
- High discounts reduced profitability  
- Consumer segment contributed highest sales  

---

## 💡 Business Impact
- Identified loss-making areas  
- Suggested discount optimization  
- Improved focus on high-profit categories  

---

## 🚀 Conclusion
This project demonstrates how data analysis can uncover business problems and improve decision-making.
